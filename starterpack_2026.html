Welcome

👋 Welcome to Nourish

Everything you need to get settled, find your feet, and start doing great work with the team. Take the first week or two to learn the ropes, meet the team, and get comfortable with the product.

Getting started

Your first two weeks

Within your first few days, you'll have a Welcome call with your line manager (ZS or Hamid), covering how Nourish works, your objectives, and introductions to the UX/Design team and your Product team PM.

You'll also have a brief intro call with HR and Finance to get the admin essentials sorted.

In-office days

We meet as a design team on the first Thursday of each month (adjustments are always communicated in advance). Locations rotate between Bournemouth, Birmingham, Bristol, London, and Oxford.

If you're travelling far or staying overnight, travel and hotel expenses are claimable — check with HR or Finance if unsure.

The UX space

We update each other on top priorities every Monday in our weekly UX Standup, or in our Teams chat. We also update Confluence as a central wiki.

We use the Team Noticeboard in FigJam for all UX/Design announcements — new team hires, upcoming design team day locations, and anything the wider team needs to know. Bookmark it early.

Team Noticeboard → FigJam Your go-to board for team announcements, new starters, and design day dates.

Design ceremonies

Alongside Product team meetings, these are your regular design team touchpoints:

Weekly · Mon
UX Standup Share your top priorities for the week. Quick, focused, async-friendly.
Weekly · Thu
Spotlight Timed design crit. Bring work you're proud of, stuck on, or want a second opinion on.
Weekly · Thu
Sandbox Senior UX/Design panel session for Pulse Design System items, blockers, and decisions.
Quarterly
Team Health Retro Anonymous team vote on how things are going, with space to discuss and improve.

121s

You'll have a fortnightly 121 with your line manager (ZS or Hamid) — a casual check-in to talk through how work is going and raise any concerns. Need something between 121s? Nudge us on Teams.

Working hours

Core hours are 9–5, Mon–Fri. We are flexible as long as communication is timely, your Product team's needs are covered, and we can reach you when needed.

Culture

Our team is supportive and passionate — we share work early, ask for help often, and are encouraged to experiment. If you're ever unsure, stuck, or overwhelmed, please reach out. There's always someone eager to help.
